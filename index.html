Understanding Change and Technology.

Learning Objectives

1. Understand and use vocabulary related to social and technological changes.
2. Use grammatical structures correctly, such as the present perfect simple and the present perfect continuous.
3. Understand written and spoken texts about the impact of technology.
4. Express opinions and discuss how technology changes our lives.
5. Write an argumentative text about the impact of technology.
