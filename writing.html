Writing

OPINION ESSAY

WHAT IS AN OPINION ESSAY?  They are formal pieces of writing where you state your opinion on a topic which must be clearly stated and supported by reasons.
PARTS

1) Introductory paragraph (you state the topic and your opinion)

2) Paragraphs 2 & 3 (you present your viewpoints supported by reasons)

3) Paragraph 4 (you give the opposing viewpoint and reasons)

4) Conclusion (you restate your opinion using different words)
POINTS TO CONSIDER

First decide whether you agree or disagree with the subject and make a list of your points and reasons.

Write well-developed paragraphs consisting of more than one sentence.

Begin each paragraph with a topic sentence which summarises what the paragraph is about.

Make use of linking words to join your ideas.

OPINION ESSAY WRITING GUIDE

PLAN USEFUL LANGUAGE
PARAGRAPH 1 Present the issue and state your opinion.

While some may claim/ it has been claimed that .. I strongly (dis)agree.

In my opinion, this is open to debate.

I firmly believe.../ In my view/ The way I see it...
PARAGRAPH 2 Give one reason with supporting details or examples.

Firstly/ To begin with/ For a start, let us look at...

The fact of the matter...

One example / instance of this is.../ Another is...

The truth is that...

Furthermore, / In addition / What is more / Another major reason...
PARAGRAPH 3 Give the second reason with supporting details or examples.

Secondly / Another factor to consider is / Another key point is that...

Furthermore, / Moreover,
PARAGRAPH 4 Give the opposing viewpoint and reasons. It is argued that.../There are people who oppose / Contrary to what most people believe, .../ Opponents of this view say...
PARAGRAPH 5  Summarise your opinion and your reasons for it

To summarise, / To sum up, / In short, / All things considered,...

However, as I see it / it is my view that...
