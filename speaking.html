Speaking

Choose one the following topics and talk about them for 8 minutes.

                             


 

1. How has technology changed the way we communicate and build relationships?
2.What are the biggest challenges people face when adapting to rapid technological change?
3. How does automation and artificial intelligence affect employment and job security?
4. Can technological change happen too quickly for society to adapt ethically or legally?
5. How has technology transformed education and the way we learn?
